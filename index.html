Welcome to Nexia!

Welcome to Nexia, a Minecraft Java vanilla server and online community dedicated to fostering a strong sense of community and collaboration. Much like the renowned Hermitcraft server, Nexia thrives on creating a vibrant, interconnected community where players can share their creativity and enjoy the game together.

At Nexia, we are committed to providing a safe, exciting, and inclusive environment for everyone who joins us. Our goal is to offer a space where players can freely express themselves, form lasting friendships, and be part of a dynamic and supportive community. We believe that a happy, engaged community is the heart of our server, and we are always attentive to its needs and feedback.

Whether you're building epic structures, embarking on adventures, or simply hanging out with friends, Nexia is the perfect place to enjoy Minecraft in a welcoming and cooperative atmosphere. Join us today and become a part of the fantastic community that makes Nexia truly special!

Server IP

Nexia Vanilla server (whitelisted): playnexia.net

Nexia is a whitelisted server, ensuring a safe and close-knit community. To join us, please apply through our Discord channel.

We run a fully vanilla server with only a handful of exceptions. For example, we use Vanilla Tweaks datapacks such as custom armor stands to enhance the gameplay experience while maintaining the core vanilla feel.

Our server operates similarly to Hermitcraft, meaning there are no land claims or protection plugins. We rely on trust among our players, but rest assured, griefing and theft are strictly prohibited. Our staff is equipped with the necessary tools to handle any such incidents efficiently.

To keep the gameplay fair and enjoyable for everyone, mods that provide unfair advantages such as hacked clients or x-ray are forbidden. However, quality-of-life mods like minimaps and statistics (e.g. HorseStats or PandaInfo) are allowed.

Discord

Our Discord server is the official channel of communication for Nexia. Stay updated with all the latest news and updates about the server, as we post everything you need to know there. It's the best way to stay connected and informed about what's happening in Nexia. It is also the place where you can apply for the whitelist and become a part of our vibrant community.

If you have any questions or issues, our staff is readily available on Discord to assist you. This ensures that you always have the support you need to have the best experience possible. Even if you are not on the whitelist yet, you can still interact with the server chat through Discord. This allows you to get a feel of our community and start making friends before you even join the game.
